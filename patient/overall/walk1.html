좌/우 GCT 밸런스 설명
Ground Contact Time (GCT)

GCT refers a time when both feet touch on the ground. GCT balance is a ratio of each time, and if it is within 51.2%, it is considered a normal balance.

The bigger GCT balance difference gets, the more left-right imbalance shows.

보행 길이(cm) 설명
보행 길이 (Gait Length)
1. Step Length

Step length is the distance between two feet when both feet are stepping at the same time, meaning the distance between the heel of one foot and the heel of the opposite foot.

2. Stride Length

The stride length is the distance between the initial stance phase contact point of one foot and the end swing phase touch point of the same foot as the distance between the stance and swing phases of the entire gait cycle.

보행 주기(%) 설명
보행 주기 (Gait Cycle)
1. Stance / Swing

The gait cycle is largely classified into ground-contact stance and swing. A 60:40 ratio is typical for this cycle.

2. Double / Single Support in Period of Stance

Stance cycle is largely divided into double support for stepping on both feet at the same time and single support for stepping with only one foot. The ratio is usually 20:40.

Double support is divided into the load response section that occurs at the start of the stance and the pre-swing section that occurs when the stance ends. A ratio of 10:10 is typical.

3. Analysis Point

There should be no significant difference in the ratio of left to right feet for each gait cycle. In particular, when gait stability is poor or balance ability is poor, the ratio of double support becomes longer.
